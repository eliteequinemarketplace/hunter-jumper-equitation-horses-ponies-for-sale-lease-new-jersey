New Jersey occupies a uniquely strategic position in the East Coast hunter jumper market — centrally located between New York, Philadelphia, and Washington DC, with easy access to every major East Coast competition venue from HITS Saugerties through Capital Challenge and WIHS. NJ-based horses can compete at the full range of Mid-Atlantic and East Coast shows within a short drive, making New Jersey one of the most competition-accessible locations in the country for hunter jumper horses.

Gladstone, NJ — The USET Foundation & Talent Search East

Gladstone, New Jersey is one of the most historically significant addresses in American equestrian sport — home to the USET Foundation Headquarters, the venue for the USEF Show Jumping Talent Search Finals East. The Talent Search Finals East at Gladstone is one of the most prestigious equitation finals in the country, and NJ-based horses competing in the Talent Search program are part of a long and storied tradition of American equestrian excellence.

Princeton & The Mid-Atlantic Circuit

Princeton, NJ hosts the USHJA National Hunter Derby North Regional Championship — one of six national regional championships on the IHD calendar. The Mid-Atlantic hunter jumper circuit based around New Jersey connects riders with major competition opportunities at every level, from local schooling shows through Premier-rated events that qualify for national finals.
